How can I apply for One Credit Card?

You can apply directly through our website by clicking on "Apply Now" or via the mobile app available on iOS and Android.

Is this a contactless card?

Yes, the One Credit Card is a contactless card enabled with NFC for fast, secure tap payments.

What is a virtual credit card?

A virtual credit card is a digital version of your physical card that can be used for online purchases securely.

Can I change my email address?

Yes, you can update your email address through the profile section in your OneCard app.

What should I do if I’ve lost or misplaced my One Credit Card?

Immediately block your card through the app and request a replacement. You can also contact our customer support for help.

My mobile phone and One Credit Card are both lost / stolen.

Please block your card by contacting our support immediately. Visit the official website from another device or call our helpline for assistance.
